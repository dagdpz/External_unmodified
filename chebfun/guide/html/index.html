The Chebfun system provides "numerical computing with functions." Many familiar MATLAB commands are extended to appropriate interpretations for functions of one variable, and Chebfun often feels symbolic but runs at the speed of numerics. More precisely our vision is to achieve for functions what floating-point arithmetic achieves for numbers: rapid computation in which each successive operation is carried out exactly apart from a rounding error that is very small in relative terms.
